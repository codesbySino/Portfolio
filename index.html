About Me

I'm a design student at OCAD University, exploring the intersection of form, function, and sustainable craftsmanship. My approach to furniture design is deeply influenced by Danish mid-century modern aesthetics—clean lines, honest materials, and purposeful details.

Through my internship at Just Be Woodsy, I've developed a philosophy that guides my practice: becoming a designer who understands manufacturing, rather than a manufacturer who designs. This perspective shapes how I approach every project, from initial concept through final execution.
